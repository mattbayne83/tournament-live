# Impeccable Design Context — Pickleball Tourney

## Design Context

### Users
- **Organizer** (Matt or a co-organizer at the scorer's table): runs the whole day from one phone/laptop, often outdoors or in a bright gym, one thumb, in a hurry between rounds. Needs zero-ambiguity controls and huge touch targets.
- **Players & spectators** (120–140 ONEOK employees and families at a charity event): read the **TV Event Board** from bleachers ~20–40 ft away, or glance at their phone between games. They want three answers instantly: *what court am I on, how long until the round ends, where do I stand.*
- Context: United Way ONEOK charity pickleball tournament (Oct 7, 2026), 8 courts, loud, social, festive.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **game-day, warm-hearted, punchy.**
Event-first United Way energy — this should feel like a fun community sports event that happens to be beautifully produced, not corporate software. ONEOK is present but secondary. Emotional goals: excitement, belonging, a little friendly competitiveness; the champion moment should feel genuinely celebratory.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Broadcast scoreboard.** The TV board is a sports-graphics package: deep ink-navy surface, massive condensed numerals in lights, team names like lower-third chyrons. It should make a company picnic feel like ESPN is covering it. The admin surface is the "production truck": high-contrast, utilitarian, sunlight-readable. Anti-references: generic SaaS dashboards, glassmorphism, neon-cyan "AI slop," timid gray admin tables.

- **Type**: Display = **Anton** (stadium jumbotron condensed, headings/scores/countdown). UI/body = **Barlow** (signage DNA, big family, real tabular figures for scores); **Barlow Condensed** for dense labels (court numbers, standings rows).
- **Palette (OKLCH)**: United Way warmth on broadcast ink.
  - Ink (board bg): `oklch(0.16 0.02 255)` — deep blue-black tinted toward UW blue
  - Surface light (admin bg): `oklch(0.97 0.008 60)` — warm paper, faint orange tint
  - Accent orange (primary action/live): `oklch(0.70 0.19 45)`
  - Red-orange (alerts, final round): `oklch(0.62 0.21 30)`
  - Gold (champions, rank 1): `oklch(0.82 0.13 85)`
  - UW blue (secondary, division tags): `oklch(0.45 0.10 250)`
  - Neutrals tinted toward hue 255 on dark, hue 60 on light; never pure #000/#fff.
- **Theme by surface**: Board + public live views = dark (broadcast). Admin + setup = light (sunlight courtside). Both fixed — no toggle; the context decides.

### Design Principles
1. **Readable from the bleachers.** Board type sizes are set for 1080p at distance: if it can't be read from 30 ft, it's decoration — cut it or grow it.
2. **One-thumb, no-oops.** Admin actions are huge, forgiving, and undoable; destructive/irreversible flows get a movement-preview or confirm.
3. **The countdown is the heartbeat.** Round timer is always the most prominent live element on every surface.
4. **Celebrate people.** Team names get typographic star treatment; the champion moment is a produced graphic, not a toast notification.
5. **Energy without noise.** Motion for state changes only (round start, rank change, champion); exponential ease-out; nothing loops idly.
